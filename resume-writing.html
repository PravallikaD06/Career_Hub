Resume Writing Guide

Here are some essential tips for creating a professional and effective resume:

  • Keep it concise: A resume should be no longer than one or two pages. Employers appreciate brevity.
  • Tailor your resume: Customize your resume for each job application by emphasizing the most relevant skills and experiences.
  • Use action verbs: Start each bullet point with action verbs to convey impact and effectiveness, such as "led," "managed," "achieved," or "developed."
  • Highlight your achievements: Instead of just listing job duties, focus on your achievements and quantify them whenever possible (e.g., "increased sales by 20%").
  • Proofread: Avoid errors in spelling, grammar, and formatting. A polished resume shows attention to detail.
